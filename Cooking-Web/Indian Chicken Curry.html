Indian Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ¾ cup coconut milk
  • 1 ½ lemon, juiced
  • 1 ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Cooking Instructions:

Step 1: Warm olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté the onion until it achieves a gentle golden brown hue, enhancing its savory and caramelized flavor profile.

Step 2: Incorporate minced garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, a bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt into the skillet. Stir the flavorful medley continuously for 2 minutes, allowing the spices to infuse and create a fragrant base for your culinary masterpiece.

Step 3: Introduce the chicken pieces, along with tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk into the aromatic mixture. Bring the concoction to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20 to 25 minutes. Allow the ingredients to meld, creating a rich and savory curry that captures the essence of the spices and tenderizes the chicken to perfection.

Step 4: Discard the bay leaf, and gently stir in freshly squeezed lemon juice and a pinch of cayenne pepper. Let the flavors meld as you allow the curry to simmer for an additional 5 minutes, ensuring a vibrant and zesty finish to your culinary creation.

Step 5: Taste hot and enjoy!
