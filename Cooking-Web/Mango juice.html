Mango Juice

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups mangos - peeled, seeded, chopped, and chilled
  • 1 ½ cups plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom

Cooking Instructions:

Step 1: Combine mangos, yogurt, milk, cream, confectioners' sugar, and cardamom in a blender; blend until smooth and frothy.

Step 2: Pour into glasses and taste immediately.
